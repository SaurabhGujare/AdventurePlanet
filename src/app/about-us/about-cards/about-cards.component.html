Our History

Established in Boston, United States , we’ve been helping 18-35’s plan their gap year & adventure holidays for over 25 years. Over 50,000 happy customers and counting!

Ultimate Trips

We offers awesome group adventures on a budget! Our tours are packed with heaps of fun day & night activities, whilst leaving time to chill out and r-e-l-a-x. Our local accommodation has been selected to offer the best experience in each destination, from top-rated 5-star hostels to floating bamboo raft houses in the jungle.

We believe in supporting the people and businesses in the places we visit and are proud to support local communities via our favourite charities. We’re big wildlife lovers and don’t ride elephants on any of our tours.
